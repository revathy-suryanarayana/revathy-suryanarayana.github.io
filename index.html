Revathy Surya Narayana

Assistant Professor | O'Donnell School of Public Health | UT Southwestern Medical Center
I am an Assistant Professor in Health Economics, Systems, and Policy at the O'Donnell School of Public Health, UT Southwestern Medical Center. I received my Ph.D. in Public Policy from the Jeb E. Brooks School of Public Policy at Cornell University.

My research focuses on health economics, labor economics, and public policy analysis.
